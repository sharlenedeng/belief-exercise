What Is a Story?

A story is the telling or retelling of a series of events, leading to a conclusion.

Now that we got that out of the way– let’s talk about how to tell a story.

To tell a story, we often use The 3 Act Structure

  • Proposal
  • Argument
  • Conclusion

Let's look at an example of what this might look like:

Act 1: Proposal

"The most embarrassing thing happened to me the other day."

Act 2: Argument

“ I didn’t get much sleep the night before but I was in class and we spent the entire day gluing paper for our project. I was talking to my classmates and I reached into my pocket for chapstick but I ended up pulling out a glue stick instead and putting it on my lips.”

Act 3: Conclusion

“The look of shock and confusion on everyone's faces was priceless but I was so embarrassed” (unfortunately this is a true story).
